Destination Guide

Juan Dolio: The Hidden Gem of the Dominican Republic

While most tourists flock to Punta Cana, Juan Dolio offers an authentic Caribbean experience just 45 minutes from Santo Domingo. With its calm waters, local restaurants, and friendly atmosphere, it's perfect for travelers who want to escape the all-inclusive bubble. Dre has called this place home for 30+ years — and he knows every corner of it.

Pro tip: The best time to visit is December to April, but Juan Dolio is beautiful year-round.

Dre's Tours

Whale Watching in Samaná Bay

Every year from January to March, thousands of humpback whales migrate to the warm waters of Samaná Bay to mate and give birth. It's one of the most spectacular wildlife experiences in the Caribbean. Dre offers private tours to Samaná, combining the whale watching with visits to local villages and authentic Dominican lunch spots.

Season: January - March only. Book early!

Dre's Tours

Los Haitises: Caves, Mangroves & Taino History

Los Haitises National Park is like stepping into another world. Ancient caves with Taino petroglyphs, dense mangrove forests, and incredible bird watching. Dre was the first officially registered ECO-tourguide in the Dominican Republic (#001) — and Los Haitises is one of his favorite places to share with visitors.

Highlights: Cave paintings, pelican colonies, mangrove boat rides.

Dre's Tours

Santo Domingo: The First City of the Americas

Just 45 minutes from Juan Dolio lies Santo Domingo — the oldest European settlement in the Americas. Walk through the UNESCO-listed Colonial Zone, visit the first cathedral built in the New World, and experience the vibrant culture of the capital. Dre combines history with local insights you won't find in any guidebook.

Don't miss: Teleférico (cable car), Mercado Modelo, sunset at the Malecón.

Travel Tips

Renting an Apartment vs. Hotel: Why Locals Recommend Apartments

Hotels are nice, but they keep you in a bubble. When you rent an apartment in Juan Dolio, you live like a local. Cook your own breakfast with fresh fruit from the market, walk to the beach without passing through a lobby, and save money for experiences instead of room service. Dre's 30+ apartments range from $32/night to luxury penthouses.

Best for: Families, long-term stays, digital nomads.
